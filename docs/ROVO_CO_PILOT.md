## Context-Aware Intelligence

Rovo doesn't show all 10 actions for every ticket. The **Strategy Intelligence Engine** filters by:

- **Board Type**: Scrum-specific vs Kanban-specific actions
- **Issue State**: Different recommendations for stalled vs in-progress
- **Priority**: High-priority tickets won't see "RETIRE CAR"
- **Story Points**: Large items trigger "THE UNDERCUT"
- **Sprint Timeline**: Days remaining affect urgency

**Result:** Only relevant actions, with clear reasoning why.
